Visitor requests a reservation

1. Visitor arrives at trinserhof.com
2. Fill in form: enters start- and end-date of their stay (on save; enter booking in our system as draft + we get an e-mail)
3. We determine the % they need to pay upfrontd; visitor is redirected to Stripe payment-page (they can pay through a lot of methods: VISA, MasterCard, SEPA, bank-transfer, Ideal, PayPal, Apply Pay, Google Pay etc: https://stripe.com/en-at/payments/payment-methods)
4. They get a confirmation of their request e-mail
5. We confirm their stay from our system? Or is it automatically confirmed if they pay?
6. A confirmed stay can NOT be changed in the calendar/our system.

Visitor cancels or wants an adjustment

1. They can reply to the e-mail we send
2. We make an adjustment in our system, either cancel or edit.
3. We can decide how much we refund of the amount the paid upfront.

- Money is transferred from guest to Stripe and then to us
- We can show the payment-status from Stripe in our system.
